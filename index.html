Dear Appa ❤️

I don't say this often enough, so let me say it clearly, in writing, so it stays: thank you.

Thank you for the long days you never complained about, for the worries you carried so quietly I almost didn't notice, and for every "no" you said to yourself so I could have a "yes."

I see it now — the years it took, the patience it asked of you, the version of yourself you set aside so I could become mine.

You were never just my father. You were my first hero, my first home, my first proof that someone could love without keeping score.

— Your son, always ❤️
